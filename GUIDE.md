# Subs — how to use it

We've got a small app for sorting who's coming to matches and sharing pitch time fairly. Takes 2 minutes to set up.

**Open:** https://subs-virid.vercel.app
No app to install, no login. Phone or laptop, both work.

---

## 1. First visit — set up your profile

- If your name is already in the list: pick **"I'm an existing player,"** type your name (it autocompletes — pick the one that matches you exactly), tap **Continue**.
- If you're new: pick **"I'm new"** and fill in:
  - **Position ranking** — rank all three positions (Attack / Midfield / Defence) 1st through 3rd. You'll mostly play your top choice; 3rd means *"I'll play it if no one else can."*
  - **Max minutes in one block** — how long you can stay on before needing a sub (e.g. 20 min).
  - **Max total minutes** — the most you want to play overall (e.g. 60 min in an 80-min match).
  - **Skill score (1–10)** — be honest. **It's hidden from everyone, including the captain.** Only the algorithm uses it to balance on-pitch quality. No one ever sees your number.
  - Tick **"I play in goal"** only if you do — keepers play the whole match and aren't rotated.
- Tap **Create profile**. The browser remembers you; you won't redo this.

---

## 2. For each upcoming match

- Tap **Matches →** at the top right.
- Tap the relevant match.
- Hit the big **"I'm coming"** button. (Or **"I can't make it after all"** if plans change.)

If you're tired or hurt and want to play less in one match — for now, edit your profile (lower the numbers) before kick-off, then change them back after. Per-match overrides without changing your profile is on the to-do list.

---

## 3. About privacy

There are no logins (so it's super easy to use). The trade-off: anyone with the link can change anyone's profile. **Don't share the link outside the team.**

The skill score is the one thing genuinely protected — the edit form never *shows* the current value (you have to retype it to change it), so even if someone opens your profile they can't read it.

---

## For the captain

After people have ticked attendance:

1. On the match page, **pick a formation** (2-3-2 = 2 def, 3 mid, 2 att, etc.) and set the **goalkeeper**.
2. Tap **Generate schedule**. The app produces a starting XI plus a substitution plan, balancing block time → total time → skill.
3. Review the timeline. If anything's missing or you want a tweak, tap **Edit schedule** — change any block, add new ones, delete.
4. On match day at the pitch, tap **▶ Run match**. You get a live clock with **audible sub alerts**: when each substitution minute hits, your phone beeps and shows a card saying "Player X off, Player Y on." Auto-pauses at half-time.
